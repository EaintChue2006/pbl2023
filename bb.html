3.Paradise Hotpot MM

Review: Singapore Hot Pot buffet is already available in Yangon! The shop name is “Paradise Hotpot MM”. It is premium Hotpot buffet. There have 4 types of packages. The difference between Premium and Set-A is included beer and not. If you do not link to drink beer, so Set-A is suitable for you. There have 8 flavors of Hotpot soup. If you are spicy lover, I recommend you have spicy Sichuan soup. Chicken Collagen soup is also not bad. Their collagen is similar with Beauty in the pot’s. To cut a long story to short, their soups are not bad. Tom-Yom taste is little bit soil. I like the most is meat quality. Their meats are so juicy. Australia Angus beef is popular in there. Seafoods are also fresh. They also have handmade meat balls. It is also one of my favorites. You should try these premium handmade balls when you go there. Side dishes are not so much special. There have Cola, Sprite and Orange for drink. You can buy other drinks of you want. They do not have so many desserts and ice cream. There just have 2 flavors of ice cream but they are tasty.
