8.Soul Sweets

Review: It is a new Korean ice cream shop which has open in Junction City. They have 3 steps of soft serve ice-cream, and you can customize as you like. For base, there has taiyaki, cup and corn. You can choose chocolate filling and custard filling for taiyaki. For flavor, there has Matcha, Dark Chocolate, Matcha Chocolate, Hojicha Milk, Strawberry, and Strawberry Hojicha milk. There have 6 or 7 kinds of topping and you can choose the one you like. For example: Marshmallows, Sprinkles, Oreos crunches, etc. Taste is not too sweet. For matcha, taste is little bitter with matcha’s smell. Also, you can get some drinks and deserts.

Address: Junction City, 3rd floor (Trendy Zone)

Phone Number: 09-788805050

To get more info: Soul Sweets
