1.Aroma2 Indian Food-Yangon Branch

Review: You can chill up and enjoy tasty india food. The goat bone is pretty cool. Compared to other countries, a fine dining experience for less than $10 is a shop that feels worth it. The side dishes which are given before eating are very unique. The main dish is the sweet and sour, like the mango sauce, and the hummus, which is dipped in beans and yogurt, and the sour is absolutely recommended. Clean service and the place is so peaceful.

5.Indian Spice-Yangon

Review: If you love to eat authentic Indian masala dishes, here is 'Indian Spice' for Indian food lovers. If I have to talk about the dishes, The first thing is that it is 100% Halal. Vegetarian dish: Paneer, Dal, Chana, Aloo, Mix Veg, Manchurian. As Non-Vegetarian dish, you can order Chicken, Mutton, Butter Fish, Prawn 4 kinds of meat, grilled, sweet and sour fried, Curry, Biryani, One set is enough for one person. Don't worry about the taste of the dish, you can ask for less spicy or masala or more. The menus are prepared after ordering, so the cooking time is 15mins-20mins. You don't have to wait so long. Also you can order from food panda application.

Address: No.(525),Ground Floor, Thein Phyu street, Mingalar Taung Nyunt township.

Phone Number: 09-985055215

To get more info: Indian Spice-Yangon

7.Punjabi-Q "The Indian BBQ Restaurant"

Review: An Indian restaurant with two types of Buffet & A la carte. They don't use much masala or oil, so its so okay to eat for those who hate much masala or oil in dishes. The next thing is the grill. The oven and the grill are at the table where you sit, and you can eat it hot. The taste of the grill is not special just normal but it's good. It's cool that grilled fish, shrimp, chicken, watermelon, and pineapple are also included. If you like Indian food, you can eat bbq & Indian food in one place. It's quite convenient. If you're going to eat a buffet, You should eat goat curry and grilled chicken curry cause they're also so tasty.

Address: 8th Floor, Times Mall, Kyun Taw Road, Kamaryut Township, Yangon.

Phone Number: 09-409700071

To get more info: Punjabi-Q "The Indian BBQ Restaurant"
